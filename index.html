How to Play

Test your skills in this classic game of Tic-Tac-Toe!

  • Player 1 uses X, Player 2 (or Computer) uses O.
  • Click a cell to place your symbol.
  • Win by aligning three of your symbols in a row, column, or diagonal.
  • Choose Quick Play for two-player mode or Vs Computer to challenge the AI.
  • Adjust difficulty in Settings for a tougher AI opponent.
