How to Play

Be the first to get three X’s or O’s in a row, column, or diagonal to win

  • Tap or click an empty square to add your X or O.
  • Play against a friend or the computer (choose easy, medium, or hard in Settings).
  • Reset your score anytime with the Reset button.
